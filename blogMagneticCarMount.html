WixGear Magnetic Car Mount and GTA Bluetooth Kit


All vehicles should have a convenient way to mount a phone. In addition, all vehicles should have an easy way to connect a phone to the car stereo via bluetooth. The WixGear Magnetic Car Mount works very well as a mount for a phone on a second generation Toyota Sienna. However, you have to do a little work to properly secure this magnetic phone holder to the dash. You need to pad the holder with thin rubber strips and secure the setup with nuts and screws to the paneling on the dash.

And since you need to remove the paneling on the dash to install the magnetic car mount, you might as well also install a GTA Bluetooth kit at the same time. Do you remember long ago when car stereos offered the option to play CDs from a 6 CD changer? Well, the stereo on a second generation Toyota Sienna has a port for a 6 CD changer. The GTA Bluetooth kit simply plugs into this port, and voila, your stereo can play music from your phone via bluetooth!

The magnetic car mount and GTA Bluetooth kit have really improved my minivan driving experience.
