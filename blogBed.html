The Best Bed for a Minivan Camper


The bed is the most important item in a minivan camper. My bed has the following features:

  • Comfortable mattress. Use the full width of the minivan, so you don't feel like you will fall out of bed.
  • Access to the storage well at the back of the minivan, under the bed.
  • Have a convenient couch mode and lounge mode.
  • Not necessary and not desirable for the couch mode and lounge mode to use full width of minivan.
  • Easy storage of bedding.
  • Lightweight bed frame.
  • Bed frame should be securely attached to the minivan.
  • Able to construct/assemble with simple tools. No fancy sewing or fancy cushions either.
  • Easy to remove from the minivan.

I built the bedframe by modifying an REI Kingdom Cot 3. To widen this cot, I first cut the cot in half. Then I used 4' wide unistrut sections to attach the left and right halves of the cot back together. The panels that fit on top of the bed frame were contructed from 1" pink insulated foam. Then I glued window screens to the top and bottom of the foam. The foam provides strength in compression, and the screens provide strength in tension. These panels are lightweight, and the panels can be moved while inside the minivan. There are several youtube videos that describe this foam/window screen construction process.
